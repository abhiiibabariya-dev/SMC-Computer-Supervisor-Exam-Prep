## EXAM STRATEGY: 120 MINUTES PLAN

| Phase | Time | What to Do |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Phase 1** (0-40 min) | 40 min | Gujarati + Computer (30-35 Q) — Do first, easiest |
| **Phase 2** (40-75 min) | 35 min | GK + English (35-40 Q) — Moderate |
| **Phase 3** (75-105 min) | 30 min | Maths + Reasoning (25-30 Q) — Takes more time |
| **Phase 4** (105-120 min) | 15 min | Review + Fill unanswered |

### Golden Rules:
1. **Never spend > 2 min on one question** — mark and move on
2. **Do easy subjects first** — build confidence
3. **Don't guess wildly** — there IS negative marking (-0.25)
4. **Only guess if you can eliminate 2 options** — then 50% chance is worth it
5. **Fill OMR sheet carefully** — misalignment = disaster
6. **Keep 15 min for review** — check for silly mistakes
