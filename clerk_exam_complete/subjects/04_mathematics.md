### Quick Reference: Important Formulas

| Topic | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| SI | (P × R × T) / 100 |
| CI Amount | P(1 + R/100)^T |
| Profit % | (Profit/CP) × 100 |
| Loss % | (Loss/CP) × 100 |
| Average | Sum / Count |
| Speed | Distance / Time |
| km/h to m/s | × 5/18 |
| m/s to km/h | × 18/5 |
| Area of circle | πr² |
| Circumference | 2πr |
| Area of triangle | ½ × b × h |
| Volume of cube | a³ |
| LCM × HCF | Product of two numbers |
