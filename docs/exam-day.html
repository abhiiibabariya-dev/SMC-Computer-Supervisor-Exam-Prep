Your Exam-Day Guide

Everything you need for the SMC 2026 written exam — admit card, what to carry, and last-minute tips so you walk in stress-free.

–
SMC Written Exam
12 July 2026 · verify exact time & venue on your call letter

🎟️ Download your admit card / call letter

The call letter is released a few days before the exam on the official Surat Municipal Corporation portal. Download & print it (2 copies recommended).

🎟️ Open official SMC recruitment portal → 📋 Recruitment dashboard / notices
Tip: log in with the same credentials you used to apply. If the link changes, search “Surat Municipal Corporation call letter 2026”.

✅ What to carry

  • Printed admit card / call letter (2 copies)
  • Original photo ID — Aadhaar / PAN / Driving Licence / Voter ID
  • 2–3 passport-size photos (same as application)
  • Blue / black ballpoint pen(s)
  • Transparent water bottle (if allowed)

⛔ What NOT to carry

  • Mobile phone, smartwatch, earphones, Bluetooth
  • Calculator, digital watch, electronic gadgets
  • Books, notes, papers, bags
  • Any metallic items / jewellery (avoid)

⏰ Reporting & timing

  • Reach the centre 60–90 minutes early — gates usually close 15–30 min before the exam.
  • Locate your exact centre a day before (do a dry run of the route).
  • Carry only what's on the admit card; there may be no locker for banned items.

📝 OMR & scoring tips

  • Fill the OMR bubble completely with the specified pen — no half marks or ticks.
  • Be aware of negative marking: don't blind-guess questions you have no clue about.
  • Do easy questions first; mark and move on — manage the clock every 15 minutes.
  • Fill roll number & booklet series correctly — errors here can cost the whole paper.

💪 Night before & final tips

  • Revise only your notes & weak topics — no new material the night before.
  • Sleep well; eat a light meal before leaving.
  • Keep your admit card + ID in your bag the night before.
  • Stay calm and confident — you've prepared for this. All the best! 🎯
