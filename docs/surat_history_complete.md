# PART 1: SURAT - COMPLETE HISTORY & FACTS

## 1.1 Ancient & Medieval Surat

| Period | Key Facts |
|--------|-----------|
| **Ancient Name** | Suryapur (સૂર્યપુર) — City of Sun |
| **Other Names** | Surat, Surath, Suvarat |
| **Founded** | Around 300 CE (ancient references) |
| **Location** | South bank of Tapi (તાપી) river |
| **First Major Reference** | 1500s — Portuguese records |
| **Mughal Period** | Major trading port under Mughal Empire |
| **British Period** | First British factory established 1612 |

### Key Historical Events:

1. **1514** — Portuguese first arrived at Surat
2. **1573** — Akbar captured Surat; became part of Mughal Empire
3. **1612** — British East India Company established first factory in Surat
4. **1613** — First British trading post (factory) officially set up
5. **1615** — Sir Thomas Roe arrived at Surat as British ambassador to Mughal court
6. **1664** — Shivaji raided Surat (first raid)
7. **1670** — Shivaji's second raid on Surat
8. **1668-1687** — Surat was the headquarters of British East India Company (before shifting to Bombay)
9. **1759** — British took control of Surat Castle
10. **1800** — British gained full control of Surat
11. **1852** — Surat Municipality established (one of the oldest in India)

### Surat as a Trade Hub:
- Called **"Gateway to Mecca"** — Hajj pilgrims sailed from Surat
- Major port for **spice trade, textile trade, diamond trade**
- Ships to **Arabia, Persia, Southeast Asia, Africa** sailed from Surat
- The word **"Surati"** became synonymous with fine textiles globally
- **Dutch, Portuguese, French, and British** all had trading posts here

## 1.3 The 1994 Plague & Surat's Transformation

| Before 1994 | After 1994 |
|-------------|------------|
| Dirty, unhygienic city | India's cleanest city |
| Poor waste management | Best solid waste management |
| Open drains | Covered drainage system |
| Health hazards | Health infrastructure improved |
| No planning | Smart city planning |

**The Story:**
- In **September 1994**, pneumonic plague outbreak hit Surat
- **~50 deaths**, lakhs fled the city
- The plague became a **turning point** — Surat decided to transform
- S.R. Rao (Municipal Commissioner) led the **cleanup mission**
- Within years, Surat went from **"dirtiest city"** to **"2nd cleanest city in India"**
- Won multiple **national awards** for cleanliness
- This transformation is studied globally as a case study

### Diamond Industry Facts:
- Surat processes **90%** of world's diamonds
- **80%** of India's diamond exports from Surat
- Employs **8-10 lakh** workers in diamond cutting
- Surat Diamond Bourse (SDB) — **world's largest office building** (opened 2023)
- Located at **Khajod**, spread over **67 acres**
- **4,700+ offices** in the bourse

### Textile Industry Facts:
- **40%** of India's man-made fabric produced in Surat
- **65,000+ power looms** and **700+ textile mills**
- Famous for **silk sarees, synthetic fabrics, embroidery**
- Called **"Silk City of India"**
