# General Knowledge for Lab Tech

## E. General Knowledge for Lab Tech (Q81-Q100)

**Q81.** SMC was established in:
A) 1947 | B) 1960 | C) **1966** | D) 1980
**Answer: C) 1966**

**Q82.** Gujarat was formed on:
A) 15 August 1960 | B) **1 May 1960** | C) 26 January 1960 | D) 2 October 1960
**Answer: B) 1 May 1960**

**Q83.** Capital of Gujarat:
A) Ahmedabad | B) Surat | C) **Gandhinagar** | D) Vadodara
**Answer: C) Gandhinagar**

**Q84.** Surat is known as the city of:
A) Lakes | B) Temples | C) **Diamonds** | D) Gardens
**Answer: C) Diamonds**

**Q85.** Surat is located on which river?
A) Narmada | B) **Tapi** | C) Sabarmati | D) Mahi
**Answer: B) Tapi**

**Q86.** WHO stands for:
A) World Human Organization | B) **World Health Organization** | C) World Help Organization | D) World Hospital Organization
**Answer: B) World Health Organization**

**Q87.** ICMR stands for:
A) Indian Centre for Medical Records | B) **Indian Council of Medical Research** | C) International Centre for Medical Research | D) Indian Committee for Medical Regulations
**Answer: B) Indian Council of Medical Research**

**Q88.** National Health Mission was launched in:
A) 2005 | B) 2010 | C) **2013** | D) 2015
**Answer: C) 2013**

**Q89.** Ayushman Bharat scheme provides health cover of:
A) Rs. 1 lakh | B) Rs. 3 lakh | C) **Rs. 5 lakh** | D) Rs. 10 lakh
**Answer: C) Rs. 5 lakh**

**Q90.** World Health Day is observed on:
A) 1 January | B) 22 March | C) **7 April** | D) 5 June
**Answer: C) 7 April**

**Q91.** Full form of AIDS:
A) Acute Immune Disease Syndrome | B) **Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome** | C) Advanced Immune Disease System | D) Active Immunodeficiency Syndrome
**Answer: B) Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome**

**Q92.** COVID-19 is caused by:
A) Bacteria | B) **SARS-CoV-2 virus** | C) Fungus | D) Parasite
**Answer: B) SARS-CoV-2 virus**

**Q93.** RT-PCR test is used for:
A) Blood grouping | B) Liver function | C) **COVID-19 / Viral RNA detection** | D) Cholesterol
**Answer: C) COVID-19 / Viral RNA detection**

**Q94.** Polio vaccine was developed by:
A) Alexander Fleming | B) Edward Jenner | C) **Jonas Salk** | D) Louis Pasteur
**Answer: C) Jonas Salk**

**Q95.** First vaccine was developed for:
A) Polio | B) **Smallpox** | C) Rabies | D) Cholera
**Answer: B) Smallpox**

**Q96.** Constitution of India came into effect on:
A) 15 August 1947 | B) **26 January 1950** | C) 26 November 1949 | D) 2 October 1950
**Answer: B) 26 January 1950**

**Q97.** Father of Indian Constitution:
A) Mahatma Gandhi | B) Jawaharlal Nehru | C) **Dr. B.R. Ambedkar** | D) Sardar Patel
**Answer: C) Dr. B.R. Ambedkar**

**Q98.** India's first nuclear test was at:
A) Jaisalmer | B) **Pokhran** | C) Barmer | D) Bikaner
**Answer: B) Pokhran**

**Q99.** Current RBI Governor (2025):
A) Shaktikanta Das | B) **Sanjay Malhotra** | C) Urjit Patel | D) Raghuram Rajan
**Answer: B) Sanjay Malhotra**

**Q100.** Chandrayaan-3 landed on Moon in:
A) July 2023 | B) **August 2023** | C) September 2023 | D) October 2023
**Answer: B) August 2023**
