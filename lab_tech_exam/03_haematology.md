# Haematology MCQs

## B. Haematology (Q21-Q40)

**Q21.** Normal haemoglobin level in adult males:
A) 8-10 g/dL | B) 10-12 g/dL | C) **13-17 g/dL** | D) 18-22 g/dL
**Answer: C) 13-17 g/dL**

**Q22.** Normal WBC count:
A) 1000-2000/cu mm | B) **4000-11000/cu mm** | C) 15000-20000/cu mm | D) 25000-30000/cu mm
**Answer: B) 4000-11000/cu mm**

**Q23.** Normal platelet count:
A) 50,000-1,00,000/cu mm | B) **1,50,000-4,00,000/cu mm** | C) 5,00,000-8,00,000/cu mm | D) 10,00,000/cu mm
**Answer: B) 1,50,000-4,00,000/cu mm**

**Q24.** ESR stands for:
A) Estimated Serum Rate | B) **Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate** | C) Enzyme Substrate Reaction | D) Electrolyte Serum Ratio
**Answer: B) Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate**

**Q25.** Which blood cell fights infection?
A) RBC | B) **WBC** | C) Platelet | D) Plasma
**Answer: B) WBC**

**Q26.** Blood group that is universal donor:
A) A | B) B | C) AB | D) **O negative**
**Answer: D) O negative**

**Q27.** Blood group that is universal recipient:
A) O | B) A | C) B | D) **AB positive**
**Answer: D) AB positive**

**Q28.** Rh factor was discovered from:
A) Rat | B) **Rhesus monkey** | C) Rabbit | D) Rhinoceros
**Answer: B) Rhesus monkey**

**Q29.** PT and APTT tests are used for:
A) Liver function | B) Kidney function | C) **Blood clotting disorders** | D) Diabetes
**Answer: C) Blood clotting disorders**

**Q30.** Reticulocyte count indicates:
A) Infection | B) **Bone marrow activity** | C) Liver function | D) Kidney function
**Answer: B) Bone marrow activity**

**Q31.** Leishman stain is used for:
A) Bacteria | B) **Blood smear (peripheral)** | C) Tissue section | D) Urine
**Answer: B) Blood smear (peripheral)**

**Q32.** Normal ESR value in males (Westergren method):
A) **0-15 mm/hr** | B) 20-30 mm/hr | C) 40-50 mm/hr | D) 60-80 mm/hr
**Answer: A) 0-15 mm/hr**

**Q33.** Sickle cell anemia is caused by defect in:
A) WBC | B) Platelets | C) **Haemoglobin (HbS)** | D) Plasma
**Answer: C) Haemoglobin (HbS)**

**Q34.** Thalassemia is most common in:
A) North India | B) South India | C) **Gujarat & Maharashtra** | D) Northeast India
**Answer: C) Gujarat & Maharashtra**

**Q35.** MCV, MCH, MCHC are part of:
A) LFT | B) RFT | C) **Red cell indices (CBC)** | D) Lipid profile
**Answer: C) Red cell indices (CBC)**

**Q36.** Normal bleeding time (Duke's method):
A) **1-5 minutes** | B) 8-12 minutes | C) 15-20 minutes | D) 25-30 minutes
**Answer: A) 1-5 minutes**

**Q37.** Normal clotting time:
A) 1-2 minutes | B) **3-8 minutes** | C) 12-15 minutes | D) 20-25 minutes
**Answer: B) 3-8 minutes**

**Q38.** Hemophilia is a disorder of:
A) RBC | B) WBC | C) **Clotting factors** | D) Platelets
**Answer: C) Clotting factors**

**Q39.** DLC stands for:
A) Direct Lipid Count | B) **Differential Leukocyte Count** | C) Diagnostic Lab Certificate | D) Disease Level Category
**Answer: B) Differential Leukocyte Count**

**Q40.** Which WBC type is most abundant?
A) Lymphocyte | B) Monocyte | C) **Neutrophil** | D) Eosinophil
**Answer: C) Neutrophil**
