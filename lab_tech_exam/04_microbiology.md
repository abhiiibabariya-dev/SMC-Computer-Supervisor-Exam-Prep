# Microbiology MCQs

## C. Microbiology (Q41-Q60)

**Q41.** Gram staining was developed by:
A) Robert Koch | B) Louis Pasteur | C) **Hans Christian Gram** | D) Alexander Fleming
**Answer: C) Hans Christian Gram**

**Q42.** Gram positive bacteria appear:
A) Red/Pink | B) **Purple/Violet** | C) Green | D) Yellow
**Answer: B) Purple/Violet**

**Q43.** Gram negative bacteria appear:
A) Purple | B) **Red/Pink** | C) Blue | D) Green
**Answer: B) Red/Pink**

**Q44.** Which organism causes Tuberculosis?
A) Streptococcus | B) **Mycobacterium tuberculosis** | C) E. coli | D) Staphylococcus
**Answer: B) Mycobacterium tuberculosis**

**Q45.** Ziehl-Neelsen staining is used for:
A) Fungi | B) Virus | C) **Acid-fast bacteria (TB)** | D) Parasites
**Answer: C) Acid-fast bacteria (TB)**

**Q46.** ELISA test is used for:
A) Blood grouping | B) **HIV detection** | C) Malaria | D) Pregnancy
**Answer: B) HIV detection**

**Q47.** Widal test is used to diagnose:
A) Malaria | B) **Typhoid fever** | C) Dengue | D) TB
**Answer: B) Typhoid fever**

**Q48.** Autoclave sterilization temperature:
A) 100°C | B) **121°C at 15 psi for 15 min** | C) 160°C | D) 200°C
**Answer: B) 121°C at 15 psi for 15 min**

**Q49.** Culture media for TB:
A) Blood agar | B) MacConkey agar | C) **Lowenstein-Jensen (LJ) medium** | D) Chocolate agar
**Answer: C) Lowenstein-Jensen (LJ) medium**

**Q50.** Penicillin was discovered by:
A) Robert Koch | B) Louis Pasteur | C) **Alexander Fleming** | D) Edward Jenner
**Answer: C) Alexander Fleming**

**Q51.** E. coli is found naturally in:
A) Skin | B) **Human intestine** | C) Lungs | D) Blood
**Answer: B) Human intestine**

**Q52.** Blood agar is used to identify:
A) Acid-fast bacteria | B) **Haemolytic bacteria** | C) Fungi | D) Virus
**Answer: B) Haemolytic bacteria**

**Q53.** MacConkey agar is used for:
A) TB | B) Fungi | C) **Gram negative enteric bacteria** | D) Virus
**Answer: C) Gram negative enteric bacteria**

**Q54.** Which test confirms HIV?
A) ELISA | B) Widal | C) **Western Blot** | D) Mantoux
**Answer: C) Western Blot**

**Q55.** Malaria parasite is detected by:
A) Widal test | B) **Peripheral blood smear (thick & thin)** | C) ELISA | D) Culture
**Answer: B) Peripheral blood smear (thick & thin)**

**Q56.** Dengue is transmitted by:
A) Anopheles mosquito | B) **Aedes aegypti mosquito** | C) Housefly | D) Ticks
**Answer: B) Aedes aegypti mosquito**

**Q57.** Normal body temperature:
A) 35°C | B) 36°C | C) **37°C (98.6°F)** | D) 38°C
**Answer: C) 37°C (98.6°F)**

**Q58.** Hot air oven sterilization temperature:
A) 100°C for 1 hour | B) 121°C for 15 min | C) **160°C for 1 hour** | D) 200°C for 30 min
**Answer: C) 160°C for 1 hour**

**Q59.** Which hepatitis virus is transmitted through blood?
A) Hepatitis A | B) **Hepatitis B & C** | C) Hepatitis E | D) All
**Answer: B) Hepatitis B & C**

**Q60.** Father of Microbiology:
A) Robert Koch | B) **Antonie van Leeuwenhoek** | C) Louis Pasteur | D) Joseph Lister
**Answer: B) Antonie van Leeuwenhoek**
