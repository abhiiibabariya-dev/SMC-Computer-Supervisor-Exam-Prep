# Lab Equipment & Safety MCQs

## D. Lab Equipment & Safety (Q61-Q80)

**Q61.** Centrifuge is used to:
A) Heat samples | B) **Separate blood components** | C) Sterilize | D) Count cells
**Answer: B) Separate blood components**

**Q62.** Spectrophotometer measures:
A) Temperature | B) Weight | C) **Light absorption** | D) Pressure
**Answer: C) Light absorption**

**Q63.** Haemocytometer is used to count:
A) Bacteria | B) **Blood cells** | C) Protein | D) Glucose
**Answer: B) Blood cells**

**Q64.** EDTA anticoagulant is used in which tube?
A) Red top | B) Blue top | C) **Purple/Lavender top** | D) Green top
**Answer: C) Purple/Lavender top**

**Q65.** Sodium citrate tube (blue top) is used for:
A) CBC | B) **Coagulation tests (PT, APTT)** | C) Blood sugar | D) Culture
**Answer: B) Coagulation tests (PT, APTT)**

**Q66.** pH meter measures:
A) Temperature | B) **Acidity/Alkalinity** | C) Weight | D) Density
**Answer: B) Acidity/Alkalinity**

**Q67.** Biohazard symbol color is:
A) Red | B) Green | C) **Orange/Red** | D) Blue
**Answer: C) Orange/Red**

**Q68.** Needle stick injury protocol includes:
A) Ignore it | B) **Wash, report, test for HIV/HBV/HCV** | C) Apply alcohol only | D) Resume work
**Answer: B) Wash, report, test for HIV/HBV/HCV**

**Q69.** PPE stands for:
A) Personal Physical Equipment | B) **Personal Protective Equipment** | C) Professional Protocol Equipment | D) Patient Protection Equipment
**Answer: B) Personal Protective Equipment**

**Q70.** Cold chain temperature for vaccines:
A) -20°C | B) 0°C | C) **2-8°C** | D) 15-20°C
**Answer: C) 2-8°C**

**Q71.** Incubator standard temperature for bacterial culture:
A) 25°C | B) **37°C** | C) 45°C | D) 56°C
**Answer: B) 37°C**

**Q72.** Which sample tube is used for blood glucose?
A) Purple top | B) Blue top | C) **Grey top (sodium fluoride)** | D) Red top
**Answer: C) Grey top (sodium fluoride)**

**Q73.** Micropipette is used to measure:
A) Large volumes | B) **Very small liquid volumes (microliters)** | C) Temperature | D) Weight
**Answer: B) Very small liquid volumes (microliters)**

**Q74.** Laminar air flow cabinet is used for:
A) Centrifuging | B) **Aseptic/sterile work** | C) Washing | D) Autoclaving
**Answer: B) Aseptic/sterile work**

**Q75.** Quality control in lab ensures:
A) Cost reduction | B) **Accuracy and reliability of results** | C) Speed of testing | D) Staff management
**Answer: B) Accuracy and reliability of results**

**Q76.** Normal urine pH:
A) 2-3 | B) **4.6-8.0** | C) 9-10 | D) 11-12
**Answer: B) 4.6-8.0**

**Q77.** Glucometer is used to measure:
A) Haemoglobin | B) **Blood glucose** | C) Cholesterol | D) Creatinine
**Answer: B) Blood glucose**

**Q78.** Benedict's test is used for:
A) Protein in urine | B) **Glucose/reducing sugars in urine** | C) Blood in urine | D) Bacteria in urine
**Answer: B) Glucose/reducing sugars in urine**

**Q79.** Microscope magnification = Eyepiece x Objective. If eyepiece is 10x and objective is 40x, total magnification:
A) 50x | B) 100x | C) **400x** | D) 4000x
**Answer: C) 400x**

**Q80.** Waste segregation - red bag is for:
A) General waste | B) **Contaminated/infectious waste** | C) Sharps | D) Chemicals
**Answer: B) Contaminated/infectious waste**
