### Q21. The SI unit of force is:
- A) Dyne
- B) Newton
- C) Joule
- D) Watt

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Newton (N) is the SI unit of force. 1 N = 1 kg⋅m/s².*

---

### Q22. IS code for plain cement concrete is:
- A) IS 456
- B) IS 516
- C) IS 383
- D) IS 269

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: IS 456:2000 is the code for Plain and Reinforced Concrete.*

---

### Q23. Standard brick size in India is:
- A) 19×9×9 cm
- B) 23×11.5×7.5 cm
- C) 20×10×10 cm
- D) 25×12×8 cm

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: Standard brick size in India is 19×9×9 cm (nominal 20×10×10 cm with mortar).*

---

### Q24. Grade of concrete M25 means:
- A) Mix ratio 1:2:5
- B) Compressive strength 25 MPa
- C) 25% cement content
- D) 25 mm aggregate

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: M25 means minimum compressive strength of 25 MPa (N/mm²) at 28 days.*

---

### Q25. The water-cement ratio for M20 concrete:
- A) 0.40
