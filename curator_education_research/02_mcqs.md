### Q1. Surat is located on the bank of which river?
- A) Sabarmati
- B) Tapi
- C) Narmada
- D) Mahi

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Surat is situated on the bank of Tapi (Tapti) river.*

### Q2. Gujarat was formed on:
- A) 1 May 1960
- B) 15 August 1947
- C) 26 January 1950
- D) 1 November 1956

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: Gujarat state was formed on 1 May 1960 from the former Bombay State.*

---

### Q3. The capital of Gujarat is:
- A) Surat
- B) Ahmedabad
- C) Gandhinagar
- D) Vadodara

*Explanation: Gandhinagar is the capital of Gujarat state.*
