# GK & Current Affairs MCQs

## A. General Knowledge & Current Affairs (Q1-Q30)

**Q1.** Who is the current Prime Minister of India?
A) Rahul Gandhi | B) **Narendra Modi** | C) Amit Shah | D) Rajnath Singh
**Answer: B) Narendra Modi**

**Q2.** National game of India:
A) Cricket | B) **Hockey** | C) Football | D) Kabaddi
**Answer: B) Hockey**

**Q3.** National bird of India:
A) Sparrow | B) Parrot | C) **Peacock** | D) Eagle
**Answer: C) Peacock**

**Q4.** Who is known as "Father of the Nation"?
A) Nehru | B) Patel | C) **Mahatma Gandhi** | D) Ambedkar
**Answer: C) Mahatma Gandhi**

**Q5.** Independence Day of India:
A) 26 January | B) **15 August** | C) 2 October | D) 26 November
**Answer: B) 15 August**

**Q6.** Republic Day is celebrated on:
A) 15 August | B) **26 January** | C) 2 October | D) 14 November
**Answer: B) 26 January**

**Q7.** National anthem of India was written by:
A) Bankim Chandra | B) **Rabindranath Tagore** | C) Mahatma Gandhi | D) Sarojini Naidu
**Answer: B) Rabindranath Tagore**

**Q8.** National song "Vande Mataram" was written by:
A) Tagore | B) **Bankim Chandra Chatterjee** | C) Iqbal | D) Premchand
**Answer: B) Bankim Chandra Chatterjee**

**Q9.** Currency of India:
A) Dollar | B) Pound | C) **Rupee** | D) Yen
**Answer: C) Rupee**

**Q10.** How many states are there in India?
A) 26 | B) **28** | C) 29 | D) 30
**Answer: B) 28**

**Q11.** Largest state of India by area:
A) Madhya Pradesh | B) Uttar Pradesh | C) **Rajasthan** | D) Maharashtra
**Answer: C) Rajasthan**

**Q12.** Smallest state of India by area:
A) Sikkim | B) **Goa** | C) Tripura | D) Mizoram
**Answer: B) Goa**

**Q13.** Longest river of India:
A) Yamuna | B) **Ganga** | C) Godavari | D) Narmada
**Answer: B) Ganga**

**Q14.** Highest peak of India:
A) Mount Everest | B) Nanda Devi | C) **K2 (Godwin Austin)** | D) Kanchenjunga
**Answer: C) K2 (Godwin Austin)**

**Q15.** First President of India:
A) Mahatma Gandhi | B) **Dr. Rajendra Prasad** | C) Jawaharlal Nehru | D) Sardar Patel
**Answer: B) Dr. Rajendra Prasad**

**Q16.** Who gave the slogan "Jai Hind"?

**Q17.** "Iron Man of India" is:
