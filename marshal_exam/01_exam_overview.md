### Selection Process
1. **Written Exam** (100 MCQs, OMR Based)
2. **Physical Measurement Test** (Height, Weight, Chest)
3. **Physical Efficiency Test** (Running, Jump, Pull-ups)
4. **Document Verification**
5. **Medical Examination**
