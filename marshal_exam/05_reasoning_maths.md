# Reasoning & Mathematics MCQs

## D. Reasoning & Mathematics (Q71-Q90)

**Q71.** Complete: 2, 4, 8, 16, ?
A) 24 | B) 28 | C) **32** | D) 36
**Answer: C) 32 (each ×2)**

**Q72.** Odd one out: 3, 5, 7, 9, 11
A) 3 | B) 5 | C) 7 | D) **9**
**Answer: D) 9 (not prime)**

**Q73.** If CAT = 24, then DOG = ?
A) 25 | B) **26** | C) 27 | D) 28
**Answer: B) 26 (D=4, O=15, G=7 → 26)**

**Q74.** 25% of 400 = ?
A) 50 | B) 75 | C) **100** | D) 125
**Answer: C) 100**

**Q75.** A man walks 5 km North, then 3 km East. How far from start?
A) 8 km | B) 2 km | C) 5 km | D) **√34 km ≈ 5.83 km**
**Answer: D) √34 km**

**Q76.** Complete: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, ?
A) Saturday | B) **Sunday** | C) Tuesday | D) Thursday
**Answer: B) Sunday (alternate days)**

**Q77.** If 5 + 3 = 28, 7 + 2 = 45, then 6 + 4 = ?
A) 40 | B) **210** | C) 24 | D) 50
**Answer: (Logic varies) - exam tip: look at patterns**

**Q78.** Average of 10, 20, 30, 40, 50 = ?
A) 25 | B) **30** | C) 35 | D) 40
**Answer: B) 30**

**Q79.** 15 × 12 = ?
A) 160 | B) 170 | C) **180** | D) 190
**Answer: C) 180**

**Q80.** 144 ÷ 12 = ?
A) 10 | B) 11 | C) **12** | D) 13
**Answer: C) 12**

**Q81.** √225 = ?
A) 12 | B) 13 | C) 14 | D) **15**
**Answer: D) 15**

**Q82.** LCM of 6, 8, 12 = ?
A) 12 | B) **24** | C) 36 | D) 48
**Answer: B) 24**

**Q83.** A shopkeeper sells for ₹600 with 20% profit. CP = ?
A) ₹450 | B) **₹500** | C) ₹520 | D) ₹480
**Answer: B) ₹500**

**Q84.** Simple Interest on ₹2000 at 10% for 2 years = ?
A) ₹200 | B) ₹300 | C) **₹400** | D) ₹500
**Answer: C) ₹400**

**Q85.** If A:B = 3:4, and B:C = 5:6, then A:C = ?
A) 3:6 | B) **15:24 = 5:8** | C) 4:5 | D) 3:5
**Answer: B) 5:8**

**Q86.** A train 200m long passes a pole in 20 seconds. Speed = ?
A) 20 km/h | B) 30 km/h | C) **36 km/h** | D) 40 km/h
**Answer: C) 36 km/h (10 m/s × 18/5)**

**Q87.** Mirror image of clock showing 3:00 = ?
A) 3:00 | B) **9:00** | C) 6:00 | D) 12:00
**Answer: B) 9:00**

**Q88.** Find next: A, C, E, G, ?
A) H | B) **I** | C) J | D) K
**Answer: B) I (skip one letter)**

**Q89.** Pointing to a photo, Ram said "He is my mother's only son's son." Who is in the photo?
A) Ram's father | B) Ram's brother | C) **Ram's son** | D) Ram's nephew
**Answer: C) Ram's son**

**Q90.** If DELHI is coded as Cdggh, then SURAT = ?
A) RTQZS | B) **RTQZS** | C) TVSBU | D) STQZU
**Answer: (Each letter -1: S-1=R, U-1=T, R-1=Q, A-1=Z, T-1=S → RTQZS)**
