# Gujarat & Surat GK MCQs

## B. Gujarat & Surat GK (Q31-Q50)

**Q31.** Gujarat was formed on:
A) 15 August 1960 | B) **1 May 1960** | C) 26 January 1960 | D) 2 October 1960
**Answer: B) 1 May 1960**

**Q32.** How many districts in Gujarat?
A) 30 | B) **33** | C) 36 | D) 38
**Answer: B) 33**

**Q33.** Capital of Gujarat:
A) Ahmedabad | B) Surat | C) **Gandhinagar** | D) Vadodara
**Answer: C) Gandhinagar**

**Q34.** Gir Forest is famous for:
A) Tiger | B) **Asiatic Lion** | C) Elephant | D) Deer
**Answer: B) Asiatic Lion**

**Q35.** Statue of Unity height:
A) 152 m | B) 172 m | C) **182 m** | D) 192 m
**Answer: C) 182 m**

**Q36.** Statue of Unity is dedicated to:
A) Mahatma Gandhi | B) **Sardar Patel** | C) Nehru | D) Ambedkar
**Answer: B) Sardar Patel**

**Q37.** Mahatma Gandhi was born in:
A) Ahmedabad | B) Rajkot | C) **Porbandar** | D) Surat
**Answer: C) Porbandar**

**Q38.** Gujarat shares border with how many states?
A) 2 | B) **3** | C) 4 | D) 5
**Answer: B) 3 (Rajasthan, MP, Maharashtra)**

**Q39.** First Chief Minister of Gujarat:
A) Balwantrai Mehta | B) **Jivraj Narayan Mehta** | C) Hitendra Desai | D) Babubhai Patel
**Answer: B) Jivraj Narayan Mehta**

**Q40.** Rann of Kutch is famous for:
A) Forest | B) **White Salt Desert** | C) Waterfalls | D) Mountains
**Answer: B) White Salt Desert**

**Q41.** Surat Municipal Corporation was formed in:
A) 1952 | B) 1960 | C) **1966** | D) 1980
**Answer: C) 1966**

**Q42.** Surat is known as:
A) Temple City | B) **Diamond City** | C) Lake City | D) Pink City
**Answer: B) Diamond City**

**Q43.** Surat is located on which river?
A) Narmada | B) **Tapi** | C) Sabarmati | D) Mahi
**Answer: B) Tapi**

**Q44.** Surat Diamond Bourse is the world's largest:
A) Mall | B) Hospital | C) **Office building** | D) Stadium
**Answer: C) Office building**

**Q45.** Bardoli Satyagraha (1928) was led by:
A) Gandhi | B) **Sardar Patel** | C) Nehru | D) Tilak
**Answer: B) Sardar Patel**

**Q46.** Which dance is famous in Gujarat?
A) Bhangra | B) Kathak | C) **Garba** | D) Bharatanatyam
**Answer: C) Garba**

**Q47.** Navratri festival lasts for:
A) 7 days | B) **9 days** | C) 10 days | D) 15 days
**Answer: B) 9 days**

**Q48.** GIFT City is located in:
A) Ahmedabad | B) Surat | C) **Gandhinagar** | D) Vadodara
**Answer: C) Gandhinagar**

**Q49.** Gujarat Legislative Assembly has how many seats?
A) 172 | B) **182** | C) 192 | D) 200
**Answer: B) 182**

**Q50.** Sabarmati Ashram is in:
A) Surat | B) **Ahmedabad** | C) Gandhinagar | D) Rajkot
**Answer: B) Ahmedabad**
