# General Science & Computer MCQs

## E. General Science & Computer Basics (Q91-Q100)

**Q91.** Chemical formula of water:
A) H2O2 | B) **H2O** | C) HO2 | D) OH
**Answer: B) H2O**

**Q92.** Which gas is essential for breathing?
A) Nitrogen | B) **Oxygen** | C) Carbon Dioxide | D) Hydrogen
**Answer: B) Oxygen**

**Q93.** How many bones in adult human body?
A) 106 | B) **206** | C) 306 | D) 406
**Answer: B) 206**

**Q94.** Vitamin C is also called:
A) Retinol | B) Thiamine | C) **Ascorbic acid** | D) Niacin
**Answer: C) Ascorbic acid**

**Q95.** Speed of light is approximately:
A) 3 × 10⁶ m/s | B) **3 × 10⁸ m/s** | C) 3 × 10¹⁰ m/s | D) 3 × 10⁴ m/s
**Answer: B) 3 × 10⁸ m/s**

**Q96.** Full form of CPU:
A) Computer Personal Unit | B) **Central Processing Unit** | C) Central Program Unit | D) Computer Processing Unit
**Answer: B) Central Processing Unit**

**Q97.** Ctrl + C is used for:
A) Paste | B) Cut | C) **Copy** | D) Close
**Answer: C) Copy**

**Q98.** Internet full form of WWW:
A) Wide World Web | B) **World Wide Web** | C) Web Wide World | D) World Web Wide
**Answer: B) World Wide Web**

**Q99.** Which planet is nearest to Sun?
A) Venus | B) **Mercury** | C) Earth | D) Mars
**Answer: B) Mercury**

**Q100.** Largest organ of human body:
A) Heart | B) Liver | C) **Skin** | D) Brain
**Answer: C) Skin**
