## General Medicine (સામાન્ય દવા)

### Cardiovascular System (હૃદય વાહિની તંત્ર)
- **Hypertension**: BP ≥ 140/90 mmHg. Classification: Normal (<120/80), Elevated (120-129/<80), Stage 1 (130-139/80-89), Stage 2 (≥140/≥90)
- **Myocardial Infarction**: Chest pain, ST elevation on ECG, elevated Troponin. Treatment: Aspirin, Clopidogrel, Heparin, Thrombolysis/PCI
- **Heart Failure**: Dyspnea, orthopnea, PND, pedal edema. Treatment: ACE inhibitors, Beta-blockers, Diuretics
- **Rheumatic Heart Disease**: Following Group A Streptococcal pharyngitis. Jones criteria for diagnosis. Mitral valve most commonly affected

### Respiratory System (શ્વસન તંત્ર)
- **Pneumonia**: Community-acquired vs Hospital-acquired. Diagnosis: Chest X-ray, Sputum culture. Treatment: Antibiotics based on causative organism
- **Asthma**: Reversible airway obstruction. Peak flow meter monitoring. Treatment: Inhaled corticosteroids, Beta-2 agonists
- **COPD**: Chronic bronchitis + Emphysema. Spirometry: FEV1/FVC < 0.7. Treatment: Bronchodilators, Oxygen therapy
- **Tuberculosis**: Mycobacterium tuberculosis. Diagnosis: Sputum AFB, GeneXpert, Mantoux test. DOTS therapy: 2HRZE + 4HR

### Endocrine System (અંતઃસ્ત્રાવી તંત્ર)
- **Diabetes Mellitus**: Type 1 (insulin dependent), Type 2 (insulin resistant). Diagnosis: FBS ≥126, PPBS ≥200, HbA1c ≥6.5%
- **Thyroid Disorders**: Hypothyroidism (↑TSH, ↓T4), Hyperthyroidism (↓TSH, ↑T4). Treatment: Levothyroxine, Anti-thyroid drugs
- **Cushing Syndrome**: Excess cortisol. Moon face, buffalo hump, striae. Diagnosis: Dexamethasone suppression test

### Infectious Diseases (ચેપી રોગો)
- **Malaria**: P. vivax (benign tertian), P. falciparum (malignant). Diagnosis: Peripheral smear, Rapid Diagnostic Test
- **Dengue**: Aedes aegypti transmitted. Warning signs: Abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, bleeding, ↓platelets
- **Typhoid**: Salmonella typhi. Widal test positive after 1st week. Treatment: Azithromycin, Ceftriaxone
- **HIV/AIDS**: CD4 count monitoring. ART: TLE regimen (Tenofovir + Lamivudine + Efavirenz)
- **COVID-19**: SARS-CoV-2. RT-PCR diagnosis. Vaccination: Covishield, Covaxin

### Hematology (રક્ત વિજ્ઞાન)
- **Anemia**: Iron deficiency (microcytic), B12/Folate deficiency (megaloblastic), Aplastic (pancytopenia)
- **Thalassemia**: Beta-thalassemia major common in Gujarat. Diagnosis: Hb electrophoresis, HPLC
- **Sickle Cell Disease**: HbS mutation. Prevalent in tribal areas of Gujarat. Newborn screening programs

## Surgery (શસ્ત્રક્રિયા)
- **Appendicitis**: RIF pain, McBurney's point tenderness, Rovsing's sign. Treatment: Appendectomy
- **Hernia**: Inguinal (direct/indirect), Femoral, Umbilical. Mesh repair techniques
- **Fractures**: Classification, Reduction, Immobilization. Compound fractures need debridement + antibiotics
- **Burns**: Rule of 9s for BSA calculation. Parkland formula for fluid resuscitation: 4ml × kg × %TBSA

### Epidemiology Concepts
- **Incidence**: New cases in a time period / Population at risk
- **Prevalence**: All cases at a point / Total population
- **Sensitivity**: True positive / (TP + FN) — ability to detect disease
- **Specificity**: True negative / (TN + FP) — ability to rule out disease
- **Positive Predictive Value**: TP / (TP + FP)

- Crude Birth Rate: 19.5/1000 (SRS 2020)
- Crude Death Rate: 6.0/1000
- Infant Mortality Rate: 28/1000 live births
- Maternal Mortality Ratio: 97/1,00,000 live births

### Sanitation & Water Supply
- Safe water: 0.5 mg/L residual chlorine
- Horrock's test for chlorine demand
- Water purification: Sedimentation → Filtration → Disinfection
- Sewage treatment: Primary (physical) → Secondary (biological) → Tertiary (chemical)
