## 2023 Exam (25th February 2023) - Code 513

### Exam Structure:
- **Total Questions:** 100 MCQs
- **Total Marks:** 100
- **Negative Marking:** -0.25 per wrong answer
- **Duration:** ~2 hours
- **Medium:** Gujarati + English

### Key Observations from 2023 Paper:
1. **Computer section was heaviest** — Focus on MS Office shortcuts, Excel formulas, networking basics
2. **Gujarat GK was prominent** — Districts, rivers, historical places, government schemes
3. **English was moderate** — Grammar, synonyms/antonyms, error spotting
4. **Math was basic** — Percentage, ratio, average, profit/loss
5. **Gujarati was standard** — Vyakaran, Kahevat, Samanarthi/Virodhi shabd
