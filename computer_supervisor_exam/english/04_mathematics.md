## Key Formulas to Remember:
- **Profit% = (Profit/CP) × 100**
- **Loss% = (Loss/CP) × 100**
- **SI = (P × R × T) / 100**
- **CI = P(1 + R/100)^T - P**
- **Speed = Distance / Time**
- **Average = Sum / Count**
- **Area of Rectangle = L × B**
- **Area of Circle = πr²**
- **Perimeter of Rectangle = 2(L+B)**
