# Biochemistry & General MCQs

## E. Biochemistry & General (Q81-Q100)

**Q81.** pH of neutral solution:
A) 0 | B) 5 | C) **7** | D) 14
**Answer: C) 7**

**Q82.** DNA stands for:
A) Di-Nucleic Acid | B) **Deoxyribonucleic Acid** | C) Dynamic Nucleic Acid | D) Deoxyribo Natural Acid
**Answer: B) Deoxyribonucleic Acid**

**Q83.** RNA stands for:
A) Ribo Natural Acid | B) **Ribonucleic Acid** | C) Reactive Nucleic Acid | D) Radical Nucleic Acid
**Answer: B) Ribonucleic Acid**

**Q84.** Which base is found in RNA but not in DNA?
A) Adenine | B) Guanine | C) Cytosine | D) **Uracil**
**Answer: D) Uracil (DNA has Thymine instead)**

**Q85.** Enzyme that breaks down protein:
A) Lipase | B) Amylase | C) **Protease** | D) Lactase
**Answer: C) Protease**

**Q86.** Enzyme that breaks down starch:
A) Protease | B) **Amylase** | C) Lipase | D) Cellulase
**Answer: B) Amylase**

**Q87.** Fermentation is:
A) Aerobic process | B) **Anaerobic breakdown of glucose** | C) Sterilization | D) Filtration
**Answer: B) Anaerobic breakdown of glucose**

**Q88.** Yogurt is made by:
A) Yeast | B) **Lactobacillus bacteria** | C) Fungi | D) Virus
**Answer: B) Lactobacillus bacteria**

**Q89.** Bread rises due to:
A) Bacteria | B) **Yeast (CO2 production)** | C) Heat | D) Salt
**Answer: B) Yeast (Saccharomyces cerevisiae)**

**Q90.** Antibiotic resistance is caused by:
A) Vaccination | B) **Overuse/misuse of antibiotics** | C) Good hygiene | D) Drinking water
**Answer: B) Overuse/misuse of antibiotics**

**Q91.** Biofilm is:
A) A type of vaccine | B) **Community of bacteria attached to a surface** | C) A staining technique | D) A culture media
**Answer: B) Community of bacteria attached to a surface**

**Q92.** WHO recommended hand washing time:
A) 5 seconds | B) 10 seconds | C) **20 seconds** | D) 60 seconds
**Answer: C) 20 seconds**

**Q93.** SMC was established in:
A) 1952 | B) 1960 | C) **1966** | D) 1975
**Answer: C) 1966**

**Q94.** Gujarat was formed on:
A) 15 August 1960 | B) **1 May 1960** | C) 26 January 1960 | D) 2 October 1960
**Answer: B) 1 May 1960**

**Q95.** Capital of Gujarat:
A) Ahmedabad | B) Surat | C) **Gandhinagar** | D) Vadodara
**Answer: C) Gandhinagar**

**Q96.** Indian Constitution came into effect on:
A) 15 August 1947 | B) **26 January 1950** | C) 26 November 1949 | D) 2 October 1950
**Answer: B) 26 January 1950**

**Q97.** ICMR is headquartered in:
A) Mumbai | B) **New Delhi** | C) Bengaluru | D) Chennai
**Answer: B) New Delhi**

**Q98.** Swachh Bharat Mission was launched in:
A) 2012 | B) **2014** | C) 2016 | D) 2018
**Answer: B) 2014**

**Q99.** BIS stands for:
A) Board of Indian Safety | B) **Bureau of Indian Standards** | C) Basic Indian Standard | D) Bureau of Industrial Standards
**Answer: B) Bureau of Indian Standards**

**Q100.** ISO stands for:
A) Indian Standards Organization | B) **International Organization for Standardization** | C) International Safety Organization | D) Indian Safety Organization
**Answer: B) International Organization for Standardization**
