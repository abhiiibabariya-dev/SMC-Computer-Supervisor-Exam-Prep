## Exam Day Strategy

### Before Exam:
1. Sleep well the night before
2. Carry: Admit card, ID proof, pen, water bottle
3. Reach center 30 minutes early
4. Quick revision of formulas and shortcuts

### During Exam (120 minutes):
1. **First 5 min:** Skim through entire paper, mark easy/medium/hard
2. **Next 40 min:** Attempt all EASY questions first across all sections
3. **Next 50 min:** Attempt MEDIUM difficulty questions
4. **Next 15 min:** Try HARD questions (only if confident)
5. **Last 10 min:** Review answers, check for errors

### Negative Marking Strategy:
- **Confident (90%+):** Attempt ✅
- **Somewhat sure (60-90%):** Attempt ✅
- **Guessing between 2 options (50%):** Attempt ✅
- **Random guess (25%):** SKIP ❌
- **No idea:** SKIP ❌

### Target Score Calculation:
- Attempt 85-90 questions confidently
- Aim for 75+ correct = 75 marks
- Even with 10-15 wrong = -2.5 to -3.75 marks
- **Net Score: ~72-73 marks (should clear cutoff)**
