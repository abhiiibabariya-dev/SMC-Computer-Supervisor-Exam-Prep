### Q21. Double entry system was introduced by:
- A) Adam Smith
- B) Luca Pacioli
- C) J.M. Keynes
- D) Irving Fisher

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Luca Pacioli is known as the Father of Accounting (double-entry bookkeeping, 1494).*

---

### Q22. Assets = Liabilities +
- A) Income
- B) Capital
- C) Expenses
- D) Revenue

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: The fundamental accounting equation: Assets = Liabilities + Capital (Owner's Equity).*

---

### Q23. Depreciation is charged on:
- A) Current assets
- B) Fixed assets
- C) Investments
- D) Cash

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Depreciation is charged on fixed (tangible) assets due to wear and tear.*

---

### Q24. Trial balance is prepared to check:
- A) Profit
- B) Arithmetic accuracy
- C) Financial position
- D) Cash position

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Trial Balance checks the arithmetic accuracy of ledger postings.*

---

### Q25. GST was implemented in India from:
- A) 1 April 2017
- B) 1 July 2017
- C) 1 January 2018
- D) 1 April 2018

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: GST was implemented from 1 July 2017 in India.*

---

### Q26. Current ratio is:
- A) Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- B) Fixed Assets / Current Assets
- C) Net Profit / Sales
- D) Capital / Debt

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities. Ideal ratio is 2:1.*

---

### Q27. Cash book is both:
- A) Journal and Ledger
- B) Journal and Trial Balance
- C) Ledger and P&L
- D) None of these

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: Cash Book serves as both a Journal (book of original entry) and Ledger.*

---

### Q28. Goodwill is a/an:
- A) Tangible asset
- B) Intangible asset
- C) Current asset
- D) Fictitious asset

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Goodwill is an intangible fixed asset representing business reputation.*

---

### Q29. Which is not a current asset?
- A) Cash
- B) Stock
- C) Debtors
- D) Machinery

**Answer: D)**
*Explanation: Machinery is a fixed asset, not a current asset.*

---

### Q30. The full form of TDS:
- A) Tax Deducted at Source
- B) Tax Deposited at Source
- C) Total Deduction System
- D) Tax Distribution System

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: TDS = Tax Deducted at Source, as per Income Tax Act.*

---

### Q31. Balance Sheet shows:
- A) Profit or Loss
- B) Financial position
- C) Cash flow
- D) Revenue only

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Balance Sheet shows the financial position of a business on a particular date.*

---

### Q32. Bad debts are shown in:
- A) Balance Sheet
- B) Profit & Loss Account
- C) Cash Book
- D) Trial Balance only

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Bad debts are an expense shown in the Profit & Loss Account.*

---

### Q33. Salary outstanding is a:
- A) Current asset
- B) Current liability
- C) Fixed asset
- D) Revenue

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Outstanding salary is a current liability (amount due but not yet paid).*

---

### Q34. FIFO stands for:
- A) First In First Out
- B) Final Input Final Output
- C) Fixed Income Fixed Output
- D) None

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: FIFO = First In First Out, a stock valuation method.*

---

### Q35. Municipal accounting follows:
- A) Accrual basis
- B) Cash basis
- C) Both A and B
- D) None

**Answer: C)**
*Explanation: Municipal accounting follows both cash and accrual basis.*

## Additional Practice Questions (Updated March 2026)

**Q36. What is the accounting equation?**
- A) Assets = Revenue - Expenses
- B) Assets = Liabilities + Equity
- C) Assets = Liabilities - Equity
- D) Assets + Liabilities = Equity

**Answer: B) Assets = Liabilities + Equity**
**Explanation:** Fundamental accounting equation. Every transaction maintains this balance.

---

**Q37. Cost accounting is primarily concerned with?**
- A) Tax filing
- B) Determining cost of production
- C) Auditing
- D) Banking

**Answer: B) Determining cost of production**
**Explanation:** Cost accounting records, classifies, and analyzes costs to determine product/service cost.

---

**Q38. Break-even point is where?**
- A) Profit is maximum
- B) Total Revenue = Total Cost (no profit, no loss)
- C) Loss is maximum
- D) Revenue is zero

**Answer: B) Total Revenue = Total Cost (no profit, no loss)**
**Explanation:** Break-even: Sales revenue exactly covers all costs. BEP = Fixed Cost / (SP - VC per unit).

---

**Q39. Budget is prepared for?**
- A) Past period analysis
- B) Future period planning and control
- C) Current period only
- D) None of the above

**Answer: B) Future period planning and control**
**Explanation:** Budget is a financial plan for a future period — helps in planning, coordination, and control.

---

**Q40. Which ratio measures liquidity?**
- A) Debt-equity ratio
- B) Current ratio (Current Assets / Current Liabilities)
- C) Gross profit ratio
- D) Return on equity

**Answer: B) Current ratio (Current Assets / Current Liabilities)**
**Explanation:** Current ratio measures short-term liquidity. Ideal current ratio: 2:1.
