### Q1. Surat is located on the bank of which river?
- A) Sabarmati
- B) Tapi
- C) Narmada
- D) Mahi

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Surat is situated on the bank of Tapi (Tapti) river.*

### Q2. Gujarat was formed on:
- A) 1 May 1960
- B) 15 August 1947
- C) 26 January 1950
- D) 1 November 1956

**Answer: A)**
*Explanation: Gujarat state was formed on 1 May 1960 from the former Bombay State.*

### Q21. The SI unit of power is:
- A) Joule
- B) Watt
- C) Newton
- D) Pascal

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Watt (W) is the SI unit of power. 1 W = 1 J/s.*

### Q22. Which metal has highest conductivity?
- A) Copper
- B) Silver
- C) Gold
- D) Aluminum

**Answer: B)**
*Explanation: Silver has the highest electrical conductivity among metals.*

### Q23. A vernier caliper measures to an accuracy of:
- A) 0.01 mm

- C) 0.1 mm
- D) 1 mm

- A) V = IR
- B) V = I/R
